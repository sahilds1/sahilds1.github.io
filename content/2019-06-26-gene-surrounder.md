Title: Project: Northwestern PhD Thesis
Date: 2019-06-25
Category: Projects
Status: published

In order to design new experiments for collaborators researching cancer, we developed bioinformatics software that can identify genes significantly associated with a disease using a novel method that resamples genomics data (10^4-10^5 genes by 10^1-10^2 samples). 

The work was supported by the Robert H. Lurie Comprehensive Cancer Center Fishel Fellowship in Cancer Research. 

Here are Google Slides with my PhD thesis presentation: [Statistical methods for the network-based analysis of genomic data ](https://drive.google.com/file/d/11rYubHJsNbr--IsYlwTXdyT64mOQKzD9/view?usp=sharing)
